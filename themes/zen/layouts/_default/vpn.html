A VPN built into Zen

We're exploring what it would look like to build VPN functionality directly into Zen – so you get ad-blocking, tracker protection, and encrypted traffic in one app, with no extra software to install.

Why build it into Zen?

Most VPNs are separate apps that run alongside your browser or ad-blocker. That means extra configuration, potential conflicts, and another subscription to manage. Building it into Zen could change that.

  • One app, full protection

    Ad-blocking, tracker filtering, and VPN encryption in a single lightweight application. No juggling multiple tools.

  • No conflicts

    Third-party VPNs can interfere with Zen's proxy-based filtering. A built-in VPN would work seamlessly with the ad-blocker from day one.

  • Simpler setup

    No separate account, no extra app, no manual configuration. Toggle it on inside Zen and you're protected.

  • Transparent and open

    Built by the same open-source team, with the same commitment to privacy. No logs, no tracking, no hidden agendas.
